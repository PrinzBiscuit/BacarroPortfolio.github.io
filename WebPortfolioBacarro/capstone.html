Capstone Program Description

A web application which grants students to view their curriculum courses and to know what specific course that requires submission of microcredentials and course portfolio. It can create a Curriculum Vitae based from the accumulated activities and projects of the students. Lastly, is the implementation of Multimedia elements, which adds Cutscenes, Videos, Images and Audio.

Scope and Limitation

The study aims to develop a comprehensive web-based application featuring three key modules: System Administrators, Program Administrators, and Students. The System Administrators module will manage account management, including uploading and managing student and program administrator account status and their personal information. Program administrators will oversee the curriculum and courses, uploading essential data such as course codes, titles, and units. They can also create submission tasks for each course, providing crucial data for students' portfolio compilation. Additionally, program administrators can view student progression and portfolios. Students can access detailed information about their curriculum and courses, including the total remaining units for their respective curriculum. The application will also feature a module enabling students to automatically generate an e-portfolio or curriculum vitae. This e-portfolio will primarily consist of their submitted schoolwork, such as machine problems and case studies, certificates from seminars or online learning platforms, and achievements from participating in events at Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna. In regards to the limitations, the system is confined to the College of Computer and Information Science department and does not support generating General Weighted Average (GWA) since it is not integrated with the Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna database. This constraint prevents the system from acquiring live data, such as the courses students are currently enrolled in. Despite these constraints, the application provides robust functionalities to enhance the educational journey of the students.
